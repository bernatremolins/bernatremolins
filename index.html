About Me

I am a predoctoral researcher in the Department of Economics at the Universitat de Girona. My research interests lie at the intersection of labour economics, gender studies, inequality, and public policy evaluation. My work is empirical and applied in nature.

I have a strong interest in quasi-experimental methods and enjoy constructing original datasets. Currently, I am working with a survey experiment and generating a DB with web scraping techniques for my thesis.

In April 2026, I will undertake a predoctoral research stay at the University of Copenhagen.
